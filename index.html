Type Racer

How to play:

Once the game starts, type the word or phrase that appears beneath the track. For each correct word you type, the speed of the Blue car will increase. An incorrect word will, however, cause you to lose speed. The aim of the game is to beat the Red car to the finish line. Good luck!
